Hi, Hello!

My name is Alexandra Valente.


Currently, I study Multimedia Design at UCL in Odense, Denmark.
I enjoy the visual design aspects of software, thus making this education more fitting than other, more technically focused, educations. Here I have been able to mix my love for drawing and designing layouts with my interest in coding, which I got into during high school.

I dream of being hired and designing anything from websites to prints for products, both for companies and for customers. Although I focus on coding and illustration, I still enjoy the other aspects of Multimedia Design, and will gladly offer my experience and knowledge on other types of content design, such as photography and filming.
